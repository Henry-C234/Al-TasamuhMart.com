Delivery FAQs

What is your minimum order amount for delivery? +
The minimum order amount for delivery is $15. Orders below this amount can be placed for click and collect.
Can I track my delivery? +
Yes, once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS that allows you to track your delivery in real-time.
What if I'm not home when delivery arrives? +
Our driver will attempt to contact you. If you're unavailable, we'll leave your groceries in a safe place if possible, or you can reschedule the delivery for a small fee.
Do you deliver to apartments or offices? +
Yes, we deliver to both apartments and offices. Please provide clear delivery instructions when placing your order.
