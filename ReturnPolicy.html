Return & Refund Policy

Our Return Policy

At FreshMart, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, we'll do our best to make it right.

Key Points:

  • 14-day return window for most products
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Products must be unopened and in original condition
  • Refunds will be issued to original payment method

Important: Perishable goods, personal care items, and clearance products are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

1

Prepare Item

Ensure the item is in its original packaging with all tags attached.

2

Bring to Store

Take the item and receipt to any FreshMart location.

3

Get Refund

Receive your refund to the original payment method.

For large items or special circumstances, please contact our customer service team.

Refund Information

Refunds are processed according to the following guidelines:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds may take 3-5 business days to appear on your statement
  • Cash purchases will be refunded in cash
  • Gift card purchases will be refunded to a new gift card
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Note: During holiday seasons, refund processing may take longer than usual.

Return Policy FAQs

What if I don't have my receipt? +
Without a receipt, we may be able to offer store credit at the current selling price if we can verify the purchase through other means (like your loyalty card or bank statement).
Can I return perishable items? +
Perishable items can only be returned if they are defective or spoiled. Please bring them to our attention immediately after purchase for a replacement or refund.
How long does a refund take? +
Refunds to credit/debit cards typically take 3-5 business days after processing. Cash refunds are immediate, and gift card refunds are issued on the spot with a new gift card.
What if I received a damaged item? +
We apologize for any inconvenience. Please bring the damaged item to any FreshMart location with your receipt for an immediate replacement or refund. For large items, contact customer service for possible pickup.
Can I return online purchases in store? +
Yes, you can return most online purchases at any FreshMart store. Please bring your packing slip or order confirmation email along with the items you wish to return.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team is happy to assist with any return questions or issues.
